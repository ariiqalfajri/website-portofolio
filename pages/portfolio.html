Multiple Currency Classifier App

This project focuses on developing a multiple currency classifier app. It consists of two models, a single currency classifier using TensorFlow and a multi-currency classifier based on the YOLO model for detecting and classifying various currency denominations in real-time. Both models achieved an accuracy of over 80%, making the app effective in automating the process of identifying and categorizing currencies from images.
